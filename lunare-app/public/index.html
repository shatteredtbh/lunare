Our story

Made by hand.
Always.

Lunare started on a kitchen counter with a small pot and too many dried herbs. Every bar is still made the same way — slowly, by hand, with attention to every cut and cure.

01

No shortcuts. Cold-process only. We don't rush the cure.

02

Honest ingredients. We list everything. Nothing hidden.

03

Small batches. Quality over quantity, every time.

Shea butter

Unrefined. Cold-pressed from West Africa.

Olive oil

Extra virgin. The base of every bar.

Essential oils

Pure plant-sourced only. Never synthetic.

Raw honey

Local. Unfiltered. Natural humectant.

How it's made

From raw to ritual.

01

Measure

Every ingredient weighed to the gram. No guessing, no shortcuts.

02

Mix

Oils and lye combined by hand using the cold-process method.

03

Pour & cut

Poured into molds, left to set, then hand-cut one bar at a time.

04

Cure

Rested for 4–6 weeks until hard, mild, and ready for your skin.
